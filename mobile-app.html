FAQs on Mobile App Development Services

What is mobile development?

Mobile app development refers to creating an application that seamlessly runs on mobile devices. Generally, developers can create native mobile applications, hybrid mobile applications, and HTML5 applications.

Why should I choose ToXSL over another Mobile App development company?

Whether you are a startup or an established enterprise, we offer broad capabilities to deliver the best to our clients. We have over a decade of experience, robust app development, and other teams working with a legacy to support the projects . From choosing the MVP model to integrating the complex features we solve all kinds of issues.

How much does it cost to develop a custom mobile app?

Before we begin working on the project, we make a proposal with a detailed project specification, discuss your requirements and create the final quote. There are no hidden charges, and we ensure everything is clear and concise. Several factors affect your app development cost, including- features to integrate, the app complexity, the platform you choose, API integration, and more. You can contact us, discuss the requirements and get the final quote!

What makes a good mobile app?

There are quite a few things mobile app developers should offer to make it the best of the competitors. Here are a few characteristics that make your app idea successful- should be versatile, offer great UI/UX, fast loading and speed, robust customer support, and adaption to user needs.

Will you assist me with the marketing and launching of my application?

Yes, we do help with the app launch! As per marketing is concerned we have professionals and a dedicated marketing team that can help you with the promotion (marketing services are paid). We can help you with direct advertisements, social media promotions, campaigns, and more.
